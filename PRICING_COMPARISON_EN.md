## When to Choose Basic?

**Best for:**

- Single outlet businesses
- Multiple outlets that operate **independently**
- Small teams (up to 2 users)
- Businesses that don't need centralized inventory
- No requirement for mobile notifications

**You get:**

- Full sales management
- Complete purchasing workflow
- Outlet-level inventory tracking
- Role-based access control
- Daily reports with PDF export

---

## When to Choose Pro?

**Best for:**

- Businesses with **multiple outlets** sharing inventory
- Need for **centralized warehouse** management
- Larger teams (3+ users per outlet)
- Want **real-time mobile notifications**
- Growing businesses planning expansion

**You get everything in Basic, plus:**

- Centralized warehouse system
- Cross-outlet stock transfers
- Real-time push notifications
- 3 notification devices included
- 1 free warehouse included

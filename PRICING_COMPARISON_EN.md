## When to Choose Lite?

**Best for:**

- Solo operators or husband-wife teams (pasar malam, warung, gerai)
- Single stall with 50 or fewer products
- Need simple sales recording and daily summary
- Budget-conscious micro-businesses
- Spotty or no internet access

**You get:**

- Quick sales recording with cash & e-wallet
- Daily sales summary (total + payment breakdown + top items)
- Simple item catalog (up to 50 items)
- Basic stock counting & adjustments
- Fully offline — works without internet

**Data stored locally on phone (not cloud)**

---

## When to Choose Basic?

**Best for:**

- Single outlet businesses
- Multiple outlets that operate **independently**
- Small teams (up to 2 users)
- Businesses that don't need centralized inventory
- No requirement for mobile notifications

**You get everything in Lite, plus:**

- Unlimited products
- Cloud data sync across devices
- Full sales management (debt, returns, discounts, customer tracking)
- Complete purchasing workflow (QT → PO → DO → Invoice → Settlement)
- Supplier management
- FIFO costing & stock movement history
- Role-based access control
- Daily reports with PDF export

---

## When to Choose Pro?

**Best for:**

- Businesses with **multiple outlets** sharing inventory
- Need for **centralized warehouse** management
- Larger teams (3+ users per outlet)
- Want **real-time mobile notifications**
- Growing businesses planning expansion

**You get everything in Basic, plus:**

- Centralized warehouse system
- Cross-outlet stock transfers
- Real-time push notifications
- 3 notification devices included
- 1 free warehouse included

## Important Notes

- **Lite plan** stores data locally on the phone — no cloud sync
- **Users** are pooled per tenant (can work at any outlet) — except Lite (1 user only)
- **Notification devices** are pooled per tenant (can be used at any outlet)
- **1 free warehouse** is included with Pro plan
- Upgrade from Lite to Basic requires data migration (contact support)
- Upgrade/downgrade between Trial, Basic, and Pro available anytime
- **Add-ons** are not available for Lite plan

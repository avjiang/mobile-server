1. What is Delivery List?

Delivery List shows all pending deliveries that need to be completed.

How Deliveries Are Created

Deliveries are automatically added to this list when you create a Sale with Delivery type.

To create a delivery sale:

  1. Add items to cart
  2. Open the cart modal
  3. Look at the top-left corner of the cart
  4. Select "Delivery" as the sales type
  5. Complete the sale

The sale will then appear in the Delivery List, ready for delivery and confirmation.

What You Can Do

  • ✅ View customer information and addresses
  • 📱 Scan item barcodes to verify packages
  • ✔️ Confirm single or multiple deliveries
  • 💬 Contact customers via WhatsApp
  • 🗺️ Navigate to delivery addresses using Google Maps

2. Getting Started

Accessing Delivery List

  1. Open the app
  2. Tap on "Functions" from the main menu
  3. Select "Delivery List"
  4. You'll see a list of all pending deliveries
💡 Tip: Pull down on the screen to refresh the list at any time.

3. Viewing Your Deliveries

Each delivery card shows:

  • Customer Name - Who to deliver to
  • Phone Number - Tap to contact via WhatsApp
  • Delivery Address - Tap to open in Google Maps
  • Number of Items - How many items in the delivery
  • Total Amount - Total value of the order
  • Payment Status:
    Fully Paid - Customer already paid
    Collect Rp X - Amount to collect on delivery (COD)

4. Searching for Deliveries

Can't find a specific delivery? Use the search bar:

  1. Tap the search bar at the top
  2. Type the customer's name or phone number
  3. The list will filter automatically as you type
  4. Tap the ✕ button to clear and see all deliveries again

5. Viewing Delivery Details

Want to see more information about a delivery?

  1. Tap anywhere on a delivery card
  2. A detailed view will open with two tabs

Information Tab

  • Full customer details
  • Complete delivery address (tap to open Google Maps)
  • Payment breakdown
  • Any special remarks

Items Tab

  • List of all items to deliver
  • Item codes (if available)
  • Quantities and prices
  • Verification status for each item

6. Confirming a Single Delivery

Step 1: Open Delivery Details

  1. Tap on the delivery card
  2. Switch to the "Items" tab

Step 2: Verify Items

You'll see each item with one of these statuses:

  • 📦 Scan Button - Item needs barcode scanning
  • ✅ Green Checkmark - Item already verified

For items that need scanning:

  1. Tap the scan icon (QR code) on the item
  2. Point your camera at the item's barcode
  3. Wait for the beep/vibration
  4. Item will turn green with a checkmark ✅
📝 Note: Some items don't have barcodes and will be auto-verified.

Step 3: Confirm Delivery

  1. Once all items are verified (green checkmarks)
  2. The "Confirm Delivery" button turns green
  3. Tap "Confirm Delivery"
  4. Done! The delivery is marked as complete

7. Confirming Multiple Deliveries at Once

Save time by confirming several deliveries together:

Step 1: Select Deliveries

  1. Tap the checkbox on each delivery you want to confirm
  2. You can select as many as you need
  3. The title bar will show: "X selected"
  4. A floating button appears: "Confirm X Deliveries"
💡 Tip: To unselect all, tap "Clear All" (red text) in the top right.

Step 2: Start Batch Verification

  1. Tap the "Confirm X Deliveries" floating button
  2. A verification screen opens showing:
    • Overall progress at the top
    • All selected deliveries grouped together
    • Current verification status

Step 3: Verify All Items

The verification screen shows all deliveries:

  1. Tap on a delivery to expand and see its items
  2. Scan each item one by one:
    • Items with barcodes show a scan button
    • Tap scan button → Point camera → Scan barcode
    • Item turns green when verified ✅
  3. Watch the progress bar fill up as you scan
  4. Items without barcodes are already green (auto-verified)
💡 Tip: You can scan items from different deliveries in any order!

Step 4: Confirm All at Once

  1. When progress shows all items verified
  2. The "Confirm All Deliveries" button turns green
  3. Tap the button
  4. All selected deliveries confirmed together!

8. Understanding Auto-Verification

What is Auto-Verification?

Some items don't have barcodes in the system. The app automatically verifies these items for you.

How to Recognize Auto-Verified Items

  • ✅ Green checkmark appears immediately
  • No scan button needed
  • Already counted in your progress

Warning Dialog

When you open a delivery with items without barcodes:

  • A warning dialog appears once
  • Shows which items are auto-verified
  • Tap "Confirm" to continue
  • You can tap the ℹ️ icon anytime to see the list again
Example:
Delivery has 3 items:
✅ "Custom Cake" - Auto-verified (no barcode)
📦 "Nasi Goreng Special" - Needs scan
📦 "Iced Tea" - Needs scan

Progress shows: 1/3 verified immediately (custom cake auto-verified)

9. Tips & Tricks

🚀 Speed Up Your Deliveries

  • Group nearby deliveries - Select multiple deliveries in the same area
  • Scan while loading - Verify items as you load them into your vehicle
  • Use the progress bar - Quickly see which deliveries still need verification

📱 Quick Actions

Tap phone number Opens WhatsApp directly
Tap address Opens in Google Maps with directions
Pull down Refreshes the delivery list
Tap "Clear All" Deselects all checkboxes at once

🔍 Better Scanning

  • Good lighting - Scan in well-lit areas
  • Hold steady - Keep camera still on barcode
  • Clean barcodes - Wipe off any dirt or moisture
  • Correct distance - Hold phone 6-8 inches from barcode

💰 Payment Collection

Check payment status before delivery:

  • Fully Paid - No collection needed
  • Collect Rp X - Amount to collect

Payment details are in the Information tab

10. Troubleshooting

❌ "Barcode Not Found" Error
Problem: Scanned barcode doesn't match any item

Solution:
  • Check you're scanning the correct item
  • Make sure barcode is for an item in this delivery
  • Try cleaning the barcode and scanning again
  • If barcode is damaged, contact support
📷 Camera Not Opening
Problem: Camera doesn't open when tapping scan button

Solution:
  1. Check camera permissions in phone settings
  2. Close and reopen the app
  3. Make sure no other app is using the camera
  4. Restart your phone if issue persists
🔄 List Not Updating
Problem: New deliveries not showing

Solution:
  • Pull down on the screen to refresh
  • Check your internet connection
  • Make sure you're on the correct outlet
  • Contact support if problem continues

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I confirm a delivery without scanning?

A: Yes, if all items are auto-verified (no barcodes). Otherwise, you must scan all items with barcodes.

Q: What if customer isn't home?

A: Don't confirm the delivery. Contact the customer via WhatsApp or phone first.

Q: Can I undo a confirmed delivery?

A: No, confirmations are final. Contact support if you confirmed by mistake.

Q: Why are some items already verified?

A: Items without barcodes in the system are auto-verified for your convenience.

Q: Can I scan items in any order?

A: Yes! Scan items in whatever order is convenient for you.
